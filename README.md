    <h2>Introduction</h2>

    <p>
        Artificial Intelligence (AI) is one of the most significant technological
        advancements of the 21st century. It refers to computer systems capable
        of performing tasks that normally require human intelligence, such as
        learning, problem-solving, decision-making, language understanding,
        and image recognition. AI has transformed industries and continues to
        reshape how people live and work.
    </p>

    <div class="highlight">
        <strong>Did You Know?</strong><br>
        AI powers virtual assistants, online shopping recommendations,
        self-driving cars, facial recognition systems, and medical diagnosis.
    </div>

    <h2>Positive Impact of AI</h2>

    <ul>
        <li>Improves healthcare through faster and more accurate disease diagnosis.</li>
        <li>Automates repetitive tasks, increasing productivity.</li>
        <li>Enhances education using personalized learning systems.</li>
        <li>Provides smart virtual assistants for daily activities.</li>
        <li>Supports businesses with data analysis and decision-making.</li>
        <li>Improves transportation with intelligent navigation systems.</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>Negative Impact of AI</h2>

    <ul>
        <li>Job displacement due to automation.</li>
        <li>Privacy concerns from extensive data collection.</li>
        <li>Cybersecurity risks from AI-powered attacks.</li>
        <li>Bias in AI systems if trained on unfair data.</li>
        <li>Overdependence on technology.</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>The Future of AI</h2>

    <p>
        The future of AI promises remarkable innovations in healthcare,
        transportation, education, finance, agriculture, and scientific research.
        However, governments, businesses, and individuals must ensure that AI
        is developed responsibly by emphasizing fairness, transparency,
        privacy, and ethical standards.
    </p>
